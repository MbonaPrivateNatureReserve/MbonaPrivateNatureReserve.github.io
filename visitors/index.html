Visitors

Mbona is a nature reserve in the Karkloof Hills, and is home to a wide array of indigenous flora and fauna.

We welcome small groups from clubs and societies such as birders and plant enthusiasts to our beautiful reserve. If you are a club interested in coming to visit Mbona, please email Sandi on board.mbona@iuncapped.co.za to arrange a suitable day to visit. Use the about and gallery links below to find out what to expect on a visit to Mbona.

We will ask visiting clubs to make a R50 donation per person to the Mbona Conservation Fund.
